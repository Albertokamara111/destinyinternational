    Our Mission & Vision

    Destiny International Worship Center is a Bible-based, Spirit-filled church dedicated to fulfilling the Great Commission. We exist to bring people into a life-changing relationship with Jesus Christ and to equip believers for their God-given purpose.

    Founded in 2005 by Apostle Olu-Bumie Syl-Macfoy, our church has grown into a diverse community representing over 20 nations. We believe in the transforming power of God's love and the authority of His Word.

    Our vision is to see lives transformed, communities impacted, and nations changed through the power of the Gospel. We accomplish this through passionate worship, biblical teaching, and compassionate outreach.
